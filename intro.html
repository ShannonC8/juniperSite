Juniper Robotics 16449

We are a school team with nine students from the Sycamore Junior High and High School, all with varying experience levels. In fact, only one of our team members was on this team last year. Two of our team members, and our coach, are rookies this year, so the other seven of us have been working to help them learn what FTC is all about. Six of our nine members were on the Sycamore Junior High team, Aviator B.O.T.S. 8, last year, so we came into the season with some connections. Most of us, however, came into this season without much advanced knowledge about robot design, programming and building.
